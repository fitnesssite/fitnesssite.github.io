Stretching

Research studies show that stretching is one of the best and easiest ways to relieve stress - whether you do it in the office, home, or gym. It relieves tension that gets “trapped” in your muscles and releases feel-good hormones that leave you feeling refreshed and rejuvenated.

When you stretch consistently, it not only lengthens your muscles (which they love) but it also enables your muscles to work more efficiently and helps your joints move through their full range of motion. All of these benefits help decrease your risk of injury and improve your everyday functionality (your day-to-day movements). Gym and Home Stretching Routine

Stretching before or after your workouts can help your body warm up and recover properly. These stretches can be completed in the gym or on your living room floor. If you’re doing the stretches before you exercise it is important to remember that you want to “pulse”, or slightly bounce your stretches instead of holding them in a static, or fixed, position.
