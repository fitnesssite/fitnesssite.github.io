Dietary Tips and Guidelines

There's no magic amount of Protein, Carbs & Fats YOU need to build muscle but usually…

    You should start off by making carbs 40% of your total daily calories and…
    You should make Protein 40% of your total daily calories and…
    You should make Fat 20% of your total daily calories
But, depending on your specific goals, you may need to adjust these baseline figures. See the sections below for the dietary breakdowns for various fitness goals.

Those looking to build muscle should have a diet consisting of 25-50% protein, 30-60% carbohydrates, and 10-20% fats. This diet should be combined with rigorous strength training as detailed in Programs. Building muscle depends as heavily on your workouts as your commitment to your diet. Maximizing your gains will depend on how you manage your calories.

Those looking to lose fat should have a diet consisting of 50% protein, 35% carbohydrates, and 15% fats. This diet should be combined with regular, strenuous cardiovascular training as detailed in Programs. Your diet plan will be all about timing, consistency, and portion control.

Those looking to maintain their fitness should have a diet consisting of 30% protein, 45% carbohydrates, and 25% fats. This diet should be combined with regular exercise and stretching as detailed in Programs and Stretching.
