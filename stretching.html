Stretching

Did You Know?

Research studies show that stretching is one of the best and easiest ways to relieve stress - whether you do it in the office, home, or gym. It relieves tension that gets “trapped” in your muscles and releases feel-good hormones that leave you feeling refreshed and rejuvenated.

When you stretch consistently, it not only lengthens your muscles (which they love) but it also enables your muscles to work more efficiently and helps your joints move through their full range of motion. All of these benefits help decrease your risk of injury and improve your everyday functionality (your day-to-day movements).
